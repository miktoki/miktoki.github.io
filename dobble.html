Dobble mini-games

The towering inferno

Setup: 1 card facedown per player, rest in draw pile faceup

Win: gain most cards

Rules: On start flip card, spot identical symbol on draw pile card, draw card and put on top of own card

Point system: +1 point per card gained / +5 points to the player who has gained the most cards

The well

Setup: 1 card faceup in middle, split rest for players facedown pile, start by giving to prev winner.

Win: get rid of all cards

Rules: On start flip pile faceup, spot identical symbol on middle card, put own card on top

Point system: +10 points to the 1st player to get rid of all cards / -20 for last

The hot potato

Setup: each round 1 card in hand
Win: get rid of card
Rules: On start flip card faceup in open hand, spot identical symbol on opponent card, put own card on top
Point system: -5 points per lost round

Gotta catch them all!

Setup: each round 1 card in middle faceup. 1 card facedown per player

Win: gain most cards

Rules: On start flip card, spot identical symbol on middle card and any other card, put non-middle card aside

Point system: +1 point per card

The poisoned gift

Setup: 1 card facedown per player, rest in draw pile faceup

Win: gain fewest cards

Rules: On start flip card, spot identical symbol on middle card and opponents card, put middle card on top of opponents card

Point system: +20 points to the player who has gained the fewest cards / +10 for the second
